FEATURES

UTILITY

The utility feature provides a collection of commands that streamline server management. This includes commands to look up information, convert units, display weather, and much more.

TICKET

The ticket feature simplifies one-on-one communication with server members. Members can open tickets to ask questions, provide feedback, or report issues while ensuring privacy.

STATISTICS

The statistics feature offers insights into the activity on your server. You can see new member counts, messages sent, and other information that helps you understand the dynamics of your community and many many more feature.
